# Domain: Consumer And Marketing

Use this file for user-facing AI claims, advertisements, consumer disclosures, pricing, recommendations, minors, vulnerable users, and investor/public statements.

## Review Areas

- Whether users know they are interacting with AI or receiving AI-generated content.
- Whether claims about accuracy, privacy, safety, bias, security, or automation are substantiated.
- Whether pricing, recommendation, ranking, or content moderation is unfair, discriminatory, or deceptive.
- Whether minors or vulnerable users are targeted or materially affected.
- Whether generated content can impersonate, mislead, defame, or infringe rights.

## Claim Review

For each claim, require:

- Exact claim text.
- Audience: consumer, enterprise customer, investor, regulator, public.
- Evidence supporting the claim.
- Material limitations or exceptions.
- Safer wording if support is incomplete.

## High-Risk Claims

- "100% accurate", "no hallucination", "bias-free", "privacy-safe", "secure by design", "fully compliant", "proprietary model", "no human review needed".
- Claims that AI can replace professional legal, medical, financial, employment, or educational judgment.
- Investor statements that overstate model ownership, data exclusivity, or regulatory readiness.

## Recommended Controls

- Claim substantiation file.
- AI disclosure and limitation language.
- Complaint and correction path.
- Human review for high-impact outputs.
- Marketing/legal approval workflow.
- Minor/vulnerable-user review before release.
